How to generate for the first time: Open a spreadsheet program like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets. As the first column header, write "Name", for the second column, write "Username". Fill out both columns with your attendee list. Once you're happy, select the entire spreadsheet (CTRL+A), copy it, and paste it in the box below.

Now, Click the Generate QR Codes button to randomly assign QR codes to all your attendees, and finally click the save button to store everything in the system. You can also copy the text and paste it back in your spreadsheet for future reference.
